Tornadoes Dashboard

Exploration of U.S. Tornadoes from 2010 to 2015

The visualizations are based on data from the NOAA's National Weather Service.
The charts and maps demonstrate the extent of tornadoes in the U.S., the severity of tornadoes, and the states with the highest causalties.
